Viewing YFL Form Submissions

After users sign up through the YFL Form, their information is automatically captured in the system. Follow these steps to view and manage form submissions.

1 Navigate to Smartlist

Go to your CRM dashboard and locate the "Smartlist" section in the left sidebar.

2 Select "YFL Form" Smartlist

Click on the "YFL Form" smartlist to view all contacts who have submitted the form.

3 View Contact Records

All form submissions will appear in a table format with contact details, submission date, and status.

Screenshot: YFL Form Smartlist showing all contact submissions
💡 Pro Tip: Use the search and filter options to quickly find specific contacts or filter by submission date, status, or other criteria.
📤

Exporting Contacts

Export your YFL Form contacts to CSV or Excel format for external use, reporting, or backup purposes.

1 Select Contacts

From the YFL Form smartlist, select the contacts you want to export. You can select individual contacts or use "Select All" for bulk export.

2 Click Export Button

Click the "Export" button located in the top toolbar or action menu.

3 Choose Export Format

Select your preferred export format (CSV or Excel) from the export dialog.

4 Download File

Click "Download" and save the exported file to your computer.

Screenshot: Export contacts dialog with format options
⚠️ Important: Exported data contains sensitive contact information. Handle and store exported files securely according to your privacy policy and data protection regulations.
🎯

Managing Opportunities & Pipeline

Track and manage opportunities through different stages of your sales pipeline. Each YFL Form submission can be converted into an opportunity.

1 Access Opportunities

Navigate to the "Opportunities" section to view your pipeline.

2 Understand Pipeline Stages

Opportunities move through various stages from initial contact to closing. Each stage represents a step in your sales process.

3 Move Opportunities

Drag and drop opportunities between stages as they progress through your sales funnel.

4 Track Progress

Monitor opportunity status, value, and expected close date for each deal in your pipeline.

Screenshot: Opportunities pipeline showing different stages
💡 Best Practice: Regularly update opportunity stages to maintain accurate pipeline visibility. Set reminders for follow-ups to ensure no leads fall through the cracks.

Quick Reference Guide

Common Tasks:

  • View new submissions: Smartlist → YFL Form
  • Export contacts: Select contacts → Export → Choose format → Download
  • Create opportunity: Contact record → Convert to Opportunity
  • Update pipeline: Opportunities → Drag & drop between stages
  • Share form: Use QR code or direct link
