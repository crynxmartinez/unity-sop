👥 How to Process a New Member Form Submission

Viewing New Member Form Submissions

After new members sign up through the registration form, their information is automatically captured in the system. Follow these steps to view and manage new member submissions.

1 Navigate to Smartlist

Go to your CRM dashboard and locate the "Smartlist" section in the left sidebar.

2 Select "New Member" Smartlist

Click on the "New Member" smartlist to view all contacts who have submitted the registration form.

3 View Member Records

All form submissions will appear in a table format with member details, submission date, and status.

Screenshot: New Member Smartlist showing all registration submissions
💡 Pro Tip: Use the search and filter options to quickly find specific members or filter by submission date, status, or other criteria.
📤

Exporting New Member Data

Export your New Member data to CSV or Excel format for external use, reporting, or backup purposes.

1 Select Members

From the New Member smartlist, select the members you want to export. You can select individual members or use "Select All" for bulk export.

2 Click Export Button

Click the "Export" button located in the top toolbar or action menu.

3 Choose Export Format

Select your preferred export format (CSV or Excel) from the export dialog.

4 Download File

Click "Download" and save the exported file to your computer.

Screenshot: Export new member data dialog with format options
⚠️ Important: Exported data contains sensitive member information. Handle and store exported files securely according to your privacy policy and data protection regulations.
🎯

Managing New Members in Pipeline

Track and manage new members through different stages of your onboarding pipeline. Each registration can be moved through various stages from initial contact to active member.

1 Access Pipeline

Navigate to the "Pipeline" or "Opportunities" section to view your member onboarding pipeline.

2 Understand Pipeline Stages

New members move through various stages from registration to active membership. Each stage represents a step in your onboarding process.

3 Move Members Between Stages

Drag and drop member cards between stages as they progress through your onboarding funnel.

4 Track Progress

Monitor member status, onboarding completion, and next steps for each new registration.

Screenshot: New member pipeline showing different onboarding stages
💡 Best Practice: Regularly update member stages to maintain accurate pipeline visibility. Set reminders for follow-ups to ensure smooth onboarding and no members fall through the cracks.

Common Mistakes

  • Exporting duplicate members - Always check for existing members before exporting to avoid duplicates in your system
  • Forgetting to assign pipeline stage - Every new member must be assigned to the correct onboarding stage immediately
  • Missing member tags - Ensure all required tags (source, campaign, recruitment channel) are applied during processing
  • Not adding onboarding notes - Document initial contact details and next steps in the member record
  • Delayed follow-up - New members should be contacted within 24 hours of registration
✅

Expected Outcome

After completing this SOP, the new member registration will be:

  • Reviewed and validated in the Smartlist
  • Exported (if needed) for reporting or backup
  • Properly tagged with source and recruitment channel information
  • Moved to the correct pipeline stage (New Registration, Contacted, Onboarding, Active)
  • Ready for follow-up with documented next steps
  • Scheduled for onboarding activities

Quick Reference Guide

Common Tasks:

  • View new registrations: Smartlist → New Member
  • Export member data: Select members → Export → Choose format → Download
  • Move to pipeline: Member record → Assign to Pipeline Stage
  • Update onboarding status: Pipeline → Drag & drop between stages
  • Share registration form: Use QR code or direct link
