📋 How to Check Form Submissions

View and Review Form Submissions in GoHighLevel

Access any form's submissions through the Sites menu

Accessing Form Submissions

GoHighLevel stores all form submissions in the Sites section. Follow these steps to view submissions for any form in your system.

1 Click on Sites (Red Checkbox Icon)

In the left sidebar navigation, locate and click on the "Sites" menu item. It has a red checkbox icon.

2 Open the Dropdown Menu

Click on the dropdown arrow next to "Sites" to expand the submenu options.

3 Select "Submissions"

From the dropdown menu, click on "Submissions" to access the form submissions page.

4 Choose Which Form to View

You'll see a list or dropdown of all available forms. Select the specific form you want to review submissions for (e.g., YFL Form, New Member Form, Contact Form, etc.).

Screenshot: Sites menu showing the Submissions option
💡 Pro Tip: You can filter submissions by date range, status, or search for specific entries using the search bar at the top of the submissions page.
🔍

Reviewing Submission Details

Once you've selected a form, you can view detailed information about each submission.

1 View Submission List

All submissions for the selected form will appear in a table format showing submission date, contact name, email, and other form fields.

2 Click on a Submission

Click on any submission row to view the complete details including all form fields that were filled out.

3 Take Action

From the submission details, you can:

  • View the contact's full profile
  • Add the contact to a pipeline
  • Send a follow-up email or SMS
  • Add tags or notes
  • Export the submission data
💡 Best Practice: Check form submissions daily to ensure timely follow-up with new leads. Set up notifications to alert you when new submissions come in.
📤

Exporting Submission Data

Export form submissions for reporting, backup, or analysis purposes.

1 Select Submissions

Use the checkboxes to select individual submissions or click "Select All" to export all submissions for the form.

2 Click Export

Click the "Export" button in the toolbar (usually at the top right of the submissions table).

3 Choose Format

Select your preferred export format (CSV or Excel) and click "Download".

⚠️ Important: Exported data contains sensitive contact information. Handle and store exported files securely and in compliance with privacy regulations.
